About the conference.

Welcome to the 8th International Conference on Family Business and Entrepreneurship (ICFBE) 2024. We invite you to join us in the vibrant city of Iloilo, Philippines, from October 09th to 10th, 2024, for a gathering dedicated to innovation, creativity, and the entrepreneurial spirit of the youth. The ICFBE 2024 aims to bridge the gap between traditional business practices and modern technological advancements, fostering a new generation of business leaders.

The ICFBE 2024 serves as a premier platform for academics, professionals, and aspiring entrepreneurs to discuss and exchange insights into the challenges and opportunities at the intersection of family businesses and technopreneurship. This conference is a unique convergence of tradition and innovation, focusing on how young entrepreneurs can transform established businesses with new technologies and innovative approaches.
