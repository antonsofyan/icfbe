About the Conference

The 8th International Conference on Family Business and Entrepreneurship.

Welcome to the 8th International Conference on Family Business and Entrepreneurship (ICFBE) 2024. We invite you to join us in the vibrant city of Iloilo, Philippines, from October 09th to 10th, 2024, for a gathering dedicated to innovation, creativity, and the entrepreneurial spirit of the youth. The ICFBE 2024 aims to bridge the gap between traditional business practices and modern technological advancements, fostering a new generation of business leaders.

The ICFBE 2024 serves as a premier platform for academics, professionals, and aspiring entrepreneurs to discuss and exchange insights into the challenges and opportunities at the intersection of family businesses and technopreneurship. This conference is a unique convergence of tradition and innovation, focusing on how young entrepreneurs can transform established businesses with new technologies and innovative approaches.

Theme: "Empowering Creativity and Innovation: Youth and Technopreneurship"

This year's theme highlights the crucial role of young entrepreneurs in shaping the future of family businesses. It emphasizes the importance of integrating creative solutions and innovative technologies to drive growth and sustainability in the ever-evolving global market. Our keynote speakers include influential figures from the worlds of entrepreneurship, technology, and business education. They will share their insights on fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among the youth.

Over the years, ICFBE has featured participants and speakers from more than 12 different countries, and participated by keynote speakers from top universities in the world like the University of Glasgow, University of Gloucestershire, Monash University, Deakin University, University of Hawaii, University of Budapest, Edith Cowan University and others.

All papers will undergo a blind review process. Subsequently, a selection of recommended papers will be considered for publication in collaborative publishing journals, including those indexed in Scopus, as well as international and nationally accredited journals (SINTA). Any costs associated with the publication process will be borne by the author(s).
