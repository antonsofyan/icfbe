About the Conference

The 9th International Conference on Family Business and Entrepreneurship.

The 9th International Conference on Family Business and Entrepreneurship (ICFBE) 2026, a hybrid SCOPUS-indexed conference, will be conducted in Hanoi, Vietnam, from 28 to 29 October 2026. For the past eight years, ICFBE has been successfully held and has attracted academic scholars and business professionals from more than 12 nations worldwide.

Vietnam is increasingly viewed as a rising economic player in Southeast Asia. Holding ICFBE in Hanoi signals our forward-looking positioning toward markets rather than established centers. Moreover, Hanoi situates the conference at the intersection of tradition, state-led reform, entrepreneurial resurgence, and regional integration. Vietnam's economic trajectory has been driven largely by privately owned small and medium enterprises, many of which are family-controlled. Hanoi, as the political capital and an emerging innovation hub, represents a compelling laboratory for examining how family enterprises balance continuity with structural transformation.

Theme: "Family Enterprises in Emerging Economies: Innovation, Governance, and Sustainable Growth - Insights from Vietnam and Beyond"

All papers will undergo a blind review process. Subsequently, a selection of recommended papers will be considered for publication in collaborative publishing journals, including those indexed in Scopus, as well as international and nationally accredited journals (SINTA). Any costs associated with the publication process will be borne by the author(s).
