President University Students Win Gold Medal Paper Award at ICFBE 2024

Iloili City, October 11, 2024 — Donald Samuel Slamet Santosa and Mikha Marsaulina Cecyana Manurung, two students from President University, have won the prestigious Gold Medal Paper award at The 8th International Conference on Family Business & Entrepreneurship (ICFBE) 2024.

The award-winning paper, titled "Virtual Reality to Teach Historical Science for Elementary School Students," explores the innovative use of virtual reality technology as a tool to enhance history education for young learners. Their research offers a modern and engaging approach to learning, aiming to make history more interactive and enjoyable for elementary students.

Their work was recognized for its originality, practical relevance, and potential impact on educational methods, particularly in integrating immersive technologies into the classroom environment.

The achievement highlights the strong research culture at President University and reflects the institution's commitment to fostering innovation and excellence among its students.

ICFBE 2024, hosted by President University, brought together scholars, researchers, and professionals from various countries to exchange ideas and promote interdisciplinary collaboration in the fields of family business and entrepreneurship.
